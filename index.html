User-level Risk Management

Your greatest asset. Your biggest vulnerability.

Your IT is good. Well thought out firewall rulesets and AV scanning from the network perimeter to the desktop are just part of your solution. An intelligent VLAN strategy combined with up to date GPOs and the results of your latest pen-test makes you confident that things are as secure as they can be.

Meet Alice. Alice works in Marketing and does a ‘lot of email.’ When Alice sees another verification link hitting her inbox, she clicks the link because it came from the mailing list she just signed up for. The page looks ok and she fills out the form, using the shared team password they use for mailing lists. And the team email account.. LinkedIn account.. SalesForce account.. In the background, the malicious web page is efficiently serving up code to Alice’s browser, hooking into the network stack and traversing your network, from the inside. Having harvested credentials from the machine, scripts on the attack platform gain access to your corporate accounts, locking you out and leaking everything to malicious actors who may just be your competitors.

Sound unlikely? Remember, just one click. Multiply this risk by the number of staff you have from CEO to cleaner (in our experience, seniority is no indicator of a reduced chance of compromise) and the critical need for strong user awareness training becomes apparent. The power of the Internet is such that one mistake from a well-meaning or distracted employee can have devastating results for your business.

Let us help you ‘Secure the Human’, empowering and educating your people through a proven security awareness programme delivered through demonstration, Q&As and one-on-one training sessions with material than can be taken back to the workplace and implemented immediately. We work closely with your IT & HR departments at all stages of the employee relationship lifecycle, allowing you to build our techniques into your existing procedures from interview onwards.

In addition to training, we design and deploy scenario-based or individual-focussed attack simulations incorporating methods such as the one described above, testing your staffs’ resilience to manipulation by telephone, email or in person. Sometimes referred to as ‘social engineering’, these coverts tests will identify susceptibility to compromise and afford you the opportunity to invest in re-training instead of loss recovery. This can be delivered as a stand-alone service, or bolted-on to one of our other services.

Infrastructure Review

The next progression from our e-Risk service, our Infrastructure Review focuses on the technical aspects of your exposure.

Combining advanced scanning engines and intelligence-driven probing into your Internet-facing assets produces a comprehensive vulnerability assessment which we cross-reference against multiple sets of data relating to known threats and exploitable scenarios. Additionally, we can deploy the same technologies against your wireless networks, an area which is becoming increasingly exploited by cyber criminals as an effective attack vector.

We compile our results into a detailed report based on a ‘traffic light’ system of severity, integrating clear language with highly-detailed technical instructions allows this to be deployed immediately as a working document for your IT team.

We will guide you through the resolution process and can incorporate post-remediation verification options.

This is a non-intrusive service and is equivalent to the targeting stages of a cyber-attack; hardening your defences here thwarts those reconnaissance attempts and presents a clear message to the would-be attacker that you are not a soft target!

Infrastructure Assessment

Intrusive detection for the ultimate piece of mind

In Ancient Greece, the revered scribe Callimachus gave inspiration to the phrase ‘to catch a thief’. We’re fairly sure he wasn’t referring to cybercrime, but the premise remains the same – to catch (or foil) a thief, it becomes necessary to think like one. This is where our analysts have the edge - an advanced understanding of the ‘dark arts’ of technology and communications and the highest possible calibre of ethical standards places them on the leading edge of their field.

Commonly referred to as a ‘penetration test’ or ‘pen-test’ our infrastructure assessments provide the next level of assurance from a more basic review by not only identifying your weaker areas, but attempting to actively exploit them in order to gain access and privilege escalations across your digital estate. We don’t limit this to the servers on your network, attackers will look at your web site and apps, VoIP systems, laptops & BYOD devices, so we do too.

These are intrusive tests and we understand that you will have concerns regarding the impact this may have. We work closely with your management and IT personnel to ensure a bespoke testing regime designed to minimise disruption while maximising results of quantifiable value. As with all our services, we provide you with a highly-granular report that delivers immediate and measured impact to your security posture.

Protective Monitoring

For when ‘Peace of Mind’ isn’t enough..

We realise there are as many different motivators for security as there are clients. Some are seeking compliance, others an enhanced review of an existing security policy. For select clients the absolute, real-time control of every byte transiting their organisation is critical. Companies whose reputations are built upon unquestionable integrity and flawless efficiency cannot afford a mistake, even once. In response to this requirement, Secure Squared have carefully developed our Protective Monitoring package, a truly bespoke service delivering an enhanced Security Operations Centre as a Service (SOCaaS). Built from the outset with your business in mind, all aspects including budget, facilities and key personnel can be tailored to your requirements.

Following on from detailed consultations with you and an in-depth analysis of your business, we provide continuous, real-time monitoring of your data flow in both directions, giving you assurance and control. Backed by defined Service Level Agreements, our intelligent systems define patterns of regular behaviour and instantly highlight anomalies for interception and manual review by one of our analysts. A wholly managed service for organisations wishing to outsource their information security operation or complement their existing team.
