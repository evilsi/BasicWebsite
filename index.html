Secure your infrastructure your data what really matters

Within the next 5 years there will be 50 billion devices connected to the Internet, electronic information will not only drive global business, it will define our very way of life.

We can help build understanding of the cyber threat to you and your business, create resilience and ensure you protect what really matters

Our team provides innovative and cost effective solutions tailored to your businesses unique needs, ensuring that your business risks are mitigated, and providing peace of mind

How can we help?

Security Policy Review
Security Policy Review

We can work with you to ensure your current security policy protects you from the main business risks you face, whilst providing expert advice and guidance to cover any gaps found to ensure you foster a culture of both corporate and personal security

Scenario Based Planning
Scenario Based Planning

An interactive session to identify the risks to your business and their impact should the worst happen. We will explain the different attack vectors and motives that drive attackers to help build an understanding of your threat landscape

Footprint Assessment
Footprint Assessment

Using the same techniques as a malicious actor we will work with you to identify the information publicly available that would aid in social engineering attacks, with a view to exposing how vulnerable key staff could be and the risks this poses to you.

Infrastructure Review
Infrastructure Review

Carrying out a detailed evaluation of you infrastructure will help enable you to determine your current security baseline, identify any vulnerabilities present, and any associated business risks.

Infrastructure Assessment
Infrastructure Assessment

Using the latest tools and techniques available to malicious actors we will help provide true security solutions by carrying out both internal and external penetration tests, remediation activities can be identified to minimise your business risk

Protective Monitoring
Protective Monitoring

Security is ongoing process, the threat landscape is constantly evolving and we must all keep up. Our team will be able to provide on-going monitoring and assessment of your assets ensuring the risks to your business are minimised.
